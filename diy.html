DIY Skin Care Masks

Face masks are the perfect skin care treatment to help you with your skin care concerns. The right face mask can help hydrate skin, remove excess oils and help improve the appearance of your pores. They're also an excellent way to help pull out impurities. Every healthy skin care regimen includes daily use and weekly use products. You can use a face mask once a week, or you can use it more then that depending on your skin and your skin care concerns. Both men and women can benefit significantly from using a mask. Masks, especially those with tingly mint in the formulation, can help stimulate blood circulation. The process of the mask drying on your skin and beginning to harden, along with the removal of the mask, causes an expansion in the blood vessels in your skin. This ensures an overall improvement in skin tone.

Acne Prone Skin

  • Mix nutmeg with honey and milk.
  • Apply it on your face. After 30 minutes, rinse off with cold water and pat dry.

Oily Skin

  • Mix gram flour with yogurt and tumeric to make a smooth paste.
  • Now apply this past on your face.

Dry Skin

  • Mash one banana and add in little honey.
  • Apply the mask evenly on the face.
  • Leave it on for 30 minutes.

Black heads

  • For this mask you will need Elmers Non-toxic glue and loose black chorcaol, usually found in pill form.
  • Break one of the pill and add enough glue to make a paste.
  • Put it on your problematic areas for black heads and pull it off once it is dry all the way through.
